Discover the Beauty of Poetry with Our Prešeren Poetry Project

Do you love poetry? Do you want to learn more about the works of Dr. France Prešeren, the greatest Slovenian poet? If so, you will love our Prešeren poetry project. This online site is designed as an interactive library of poems, which you can browse by alphabet or category. Each poem is stored in JSON format, which enables you to easily manipulate and customize it. The site is designed using HTML, CSS and JS, which offers a modern and responsive interface. Visit this site and dive into the world of poetry.

Who Was Dr. France Prešeren? Dr. France Prešeren (1800-1849) was a Slovenian poet, lawyer, and cultural icon. He is widely regarded as the national poet of Slovenia and one of the most influential figures in the Slovenian literary history. He wrote in the Slovenian language, which was not widely recognized or used at the time. He is best known for his lyrical poems, sonnets, and ballads, which express his love, patriotism, and social criticism. He also wrote the lyrics for the Slovenian national anthem, Zdravljica (A Toast).
